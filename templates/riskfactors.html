Understanding Risk Factors

Heart disease risk is multifactorial — influenced by demographic characteristics, lifestyle choices, and underlying health conditions. Our analysis of over 319,000 patients reveals significant correlations between these factors and heart disease outcomes. Understanding these risks enables better prevention strategies and targeted interventions.
