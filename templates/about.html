About The Project

Understanding the Project

An in-depth exploration of heart disease risk factors using real-world healthcare data and interactive Tableau visualizations.

The Problem

Why Heart Disease Analysis Matters

Heart disease remains the leading cause of death globally, accounting for over 17.9 million deaths per year. Understanding the risk factors that contribute to heart disease is critical for prevention, early diagnosis, and effective treatment strategies.

This project leverages a comprehensive dataset of over 319,000 patients to identify demographic, lifestyle, and health-related risk factors — providing actionable insights through interactive data visualizations.
